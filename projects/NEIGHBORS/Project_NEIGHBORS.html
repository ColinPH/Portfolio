The Game

Take the fight to your pesky neighbors in a mayhem filled team-based online co-op battle. Assemble a team of iconic residents, attack your rival’s home with crazy items, and deploy devious traps to defend your own. Whose house will survive to rule the neighborhood?

My Contributions

Gameplay Programmer

I designed and implemented multiplayer gameplay mechanics and interactive systems within the Unreal Engine. This includes throwable traps, dynamic items, and player progression perks. I was responsible for developing and iterating on a modular tutorial framework, enabling flexible sequencing and scalable integration of new tutorial tasks. I conducted user research and playtesting, using the Wizard of Oz technique and rapid paper prototyping, to refine the tutorial content. I collaborated cross-functionally with designers, artists, and producers to ensure feature alignment with creative and technical goals.

QA

I pioneered the QA department. Setting up workflows and user testing methodologies. I created quantitative and qualitative player experience surveys, integrating telemetry data with user feedback for deeper insights. I synthesised and presented weekly UX reports, highlighting strategies and gameplay friction points. I analysed player behaviour patterns, and worked with leadership to refine core mechanics and game balance.

System Designer

I was responsible for balancing the game economy and combat systems, mapping resource flows, sinks, and player incentives. I used Machinations.io and spreadsheets to analyze and fine-tune economic and combat balance. I identified inflation issues and restructured in-game currency reward distribution and pricing models to improve progression pacing.
