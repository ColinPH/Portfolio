The Game

STRIBE is a physical card game with augmented reality. A crowdfunded project which reached 200% of its initial goal, allowing players to fight by creating combinations among the 5 different tribes with unique powers. Plays with up to 5 people.

My Contributions

Unity Developer

In this project I was responsible for the entire development of the application. Working together with the artist to communicate about the required assets. I used Vuforia then Easy AR to create the augmented reality effects. The application was released on both Android and Apple stores.

Accounting

In order to make sure the project would become and remain financially viable over the crowdfunding campaign, I created an Excel sheet to oversee all the expenses and potential earnings. This has led to a major restructuring of the stretch goals which saved the project from loosing money.
